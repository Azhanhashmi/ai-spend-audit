## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no account, no catch. The audit runs instantly in your browser.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price is sourced from official vendor pricing pages and verified weekly.
Sources are listed in our pricing data file.

**Q: Do you store my spending data?**
A: Your form data stays in your browser. We only store the audit result
(not your email) when you choose to get a shareable link.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex is a marketplace for discounted AI infrastructure credits.
This tool is free — Credex earns revenue when users with large savings
opt to buy credits at a discount.

**Q: What AI tools do you cover?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API,
Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools added weekly.
