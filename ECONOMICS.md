# Unit Economics

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Average AI credit purchase via Credex: $2,000 (conservative)
Gross margin on credits: ~25% = $500 per customer
LTV with 2 repeat purchases: $1,000

## CAC by channel

| Channel | Cost | Expected conversions | CAC |
|---|---|---|---|
| Show HN post | $0 | 5 customers | $0 |
| Cold DM (50 CTOs) | 3 hrs time | 2 customers | $0 cash |
| Indie Hackers post | $0 | 3 customers | $0 |
| Paid Twitter ads (later) | $500 | 5 customers | $100 |

## Conversion funnel

| Stage | Rate | Numbers |
|---|---|---|
| Lands on tool | 100% | 1000 visitors |
| Completes audit | 35% | 350 audits |
| Submits email | 25% | 87 leads |
| Books Credex consultation | 10% | 8 calls |
| Purchases credits | 40% | 3 customers |

Revenue per 1000 visitors = 3 × $500 margin = $1,500

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

$1M ARR = $83,333/month revenue
At $500 margin per customer = 167 new customers/month
At 3% visitor-to-customer rate = 5,566 visitors/month needed

This is achievable with:
- 1 viral HN post/month driving 3,000 visits
- SEO on "AI tool cost comparison" queries (2,000 visits/month by month 6)
- Credex email list nurture (500 visits/month)

**Key assumption that must be true:** Consultation booking rate stays above 8%.
Below 5% the funnel math breaks and we need to redesign the CTA.
